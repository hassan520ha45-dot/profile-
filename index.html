Executive Summary

Founded in 2019, MARINE Square is a specialized engineering and technology solutions provider serving Bangladesh's marine, defense, and industrial sectors. Led by experienced naval engineers, the company offers Source–Supply–Service solutions that combine equipment procurement, project execution, and technical support.

Our expertise includes marine propulsion systems, onboard machinery, workshop tools, diving and underwater services, and vessel refit support for the Bangladesh Navy, Coast Guard, and other government organizations.

Through global partnerships with manufacturers in Europe, Asia, and the USA, we ensure every product meets international marine standards for safety, quality, and reliability.

Beyond traditional engineering, we are developing IoT-enabled monitoring systems, drone solutions, and robotics training labs to promote automation, innovation, and education in Bangladesh's maritime and technical sectors.

Core Competencies & Services

Our technical team combines engineering expertise with project management discipline to deliver turnkey marine solutions — from concept to commissioning.

Sourcing & Supply of Technical Equipment: Procurement and delivery of marine, mechanical, electrical, and defense systems from globally reputed OEMs.
Ship & Boat Construction / Refit: Comprehensive fabrication, renovation, and retrofitting of naval, commercial, and riverine vessels.
Maintenance, Overhaul & Technical Support: Overhaul and maintenance of propulsion systems, auxiliary machinery, HVAC, and onboard systems.
Diving & Underwater Services: Underwater inspection, survey, salvage, and recovery by certified naval divers and PADI/US Navy–licensed professionals.
Technical Consultancy & Project Management: Equipment selection, installation, commissioning, and after-sales support — delivered with engineering precision and efficiency.
